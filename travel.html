Wedding Location

We are getting married at Wisteria Hall in the Washington Park Arboretum.

The wedding is on May 24, 2026, the Sunday of Memorial Day weekend.

We have a hotel block reserved at the Kimpton Hotel Vintage. Rooms are $189/night + tax + $30/night for parking. You can book the hotel at this link. Note that this shows a daily amenity fee but per our contract with the hotel you will not actually be charged this fee. You can adjust what days you are booking for by clicking "edit".
If you are interested in a higher-end hotel, we also highly recommend the Inn at the Market.

If you're flying in from out of town, we recommend renting a car.



Saturday Pre-wedding BBQ and Hike at Seward Park (Optional)

We are hosting a BBQ on Saturday, May 23rd starting at 11:00 AM atSeward Park Picnic Structure #2. This space features a beautiful but hilly park on Lake Washington; make sure to dress for the terrain. At 1:00i PM, we will also be doing a small hike around Seward Park for whoever is interested.



Monday Post-wedding Hike at Lake Keechelus (Optional)

We are hosting a hike around Lake Keechelus on Monday, May 25th at 2:00 PM. It features a flat, wide trail with pretty views where we can amble, chat, and destress after the wedding. We will be meeting at the Snoqualmie Tunnel Parking Lot. We will rendezvous at the parking lot and wait for all expected guests so the actual start time of the hike may end up being a bit earlier or later. We recommend leaving downtown Seattle at approximately 1:00 PM since it is about 1 hour away from Pike's Place Market. Please let us know if you are interested in going to this hike but do not have proper transportation.
